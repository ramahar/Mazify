Maze Generation

Maze generation algorithms are automated methods for the creation of mazes.

A maze can be generated by starting with a predetermined arrangement of cells (most commonly a rectangular grid) with wall sites between them. This arrangement can be considered as a connected graph, where the nodes represent cells in the grid and the edges represent the walls of these cells.
